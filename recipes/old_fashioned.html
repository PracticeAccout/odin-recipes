Old Fashioned

Description

The old fashioned is a cocktail made by muddling sugar with bitters
and water, adding whiskey, and garnishing with orange twist or zest and
a cocktail cherry. It is traditionally served in an old fashioned glass,
which predated the cocktail.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz bourbon or rye whiskey
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 1 sugar cube or 1 tsp sugar
  • Orange twist garnish
