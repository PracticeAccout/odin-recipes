Margarita

Description

A margarita is a cocktail consisting of tequila, orange liqueur, and lime juice
served with salt on the rim of the glass. The drink is served shaken with ice,
blended with ice, or without ice

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz silver tequila
  • 1 oz Cointreau
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • Salt for the rim
